Hypothesis Validation

Six hypotheses tested against 1,017 NL responses. All confirmed.

H1 Confirmed

Strategy exists but doesn't drive action

73% have a strategy, but only 15% can fully measure impact. 72% still in maturity stages 1-2.

15% can fully measure L&D impact
H2 Confirmed

Culture fails on structure, not motivation

Managers: 66% say active culture. Employees: only 50%. 16pp gap. 58% say employees self-direct learning.

16pp culture perception gap
H3 Confirmed

Manager perception gap

72% of managers say feedback leads to development. Only 55% of employees agree. 17pp gap.

17pp manager-employee gap
H4 Confirmed

Skill transparency crisis

8.5% full skill transparency
H5 Confirmed

Upskilling recognized but not embedded
